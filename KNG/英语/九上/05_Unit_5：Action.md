## 知识点3：语法——宾语从句（一）

### 内容
**that 引导的宾语从句**：
- that 无意义，在从句中不作成分，可省略
- 例句：
  - I believe (that) he is honest.
  - She told me (that) she would be late.

**whether/if 引导的宾语从句**：
- whether/if 意为"是否"
- 例句：
  - I don't know whether/if he will come.
  - Can you tell me whether/if it is true?

**whether 与 if 的区别**：
1. whether 可与 or not 直接连用：I don't know whether or not he will come.
2. 介词后只用 whether：It depends on whether we have enough money.
3. whether 可引导不定式：I don't know whether to accept his invitation.
4. 强调两方面的选择时用 whether：Whether he will come, I'm not sure.

## 知识点5：写作——电影评论

### 内容
**写作框架**：
1. 引言：介绍电影名称、类型、基本信息
2. 主体：评价情节、表演、特效等
3. 结尾：总结评价，推荐与否

**常用表达**：
- ... is a ... film directed by ...
- The film tells the story of ...
- The plot is exciting/moving/boring.
- The acting is excellent/terrible.
- The special effects are amazing.
- I would recommend this film to ...
- In conclusion, ... is worth watching.
- If you like ..., you will enjoy this film.
