## 知识点2：重点句型

### 内容

- 特殊疑问句的直接引语变间接引语

## 知识点3：语法——直接引语和间接引语（二）

### 内容
**特殊疑问句变间接引语**：
- 保留特殊疑问词
- 语序变为陈述语序（主语 + 谓语）
- 例句：
  - "Where does she live?" I asked. → I asked where she lived.
  - "What happened?" he wondered. → He wondered what had happened.
  - "Who is coming?" she asked. → She asked who was coming.

**祈使句变间接引语**：
- 肯定祈使句：tell/ask/order sb. to do sth.
  - "Sit down," he said. → He told me to sit down.
- 否定祈使句：tell/warn sb. not to do sth.
  - "Don't touch it," she said. → She warned me not to touch it.

**各种祈使句的引述动词**：
- tell/ask：一般的请求或命令
- order/command：命令
- warn：警告
- advise：建议
- beg：恳求
- invite：邀请

### 注意事项

- 祈使句的引述动词要根据语气选择
- 注意 let's 开头的祈使句：suggest/propose + doing

**核心词汇**：ending, surprising, unexpected, plot, twist, suspense, reveal ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
