## 知识点3：语法——直接引语和间接引语（一）

### 内容
**陈述句变间接引语**：
- 引述动词：say, tell, explain 等
- 结构：主语 + said/told sb. + (that) + 陈述句
- 例句：
  - He said, "I am tired." → He said (that) he was tired.
  - She told me, "I will call you tomorrow." → She told me (that) she would call me the next day.

**一般疑问句变间接引语**：
- 引述动词：ask, wonder, want to know 等
- 结构：主语 + asked + if/whether + 陈述句
- 例句：
  - He asked, "Are you ready?" → He asked if/whether I was ready.
  - She wondered, "Does he know the truth?" → She wondered if/whether he knew the truth.

**变化规则**：
1. 人称变化
2. 时态变化（主句过去时，从句相应变化）
3. 指示代词变化：this → that, these → those
4. 时间状语变化：now → then, today → that day, tomorrow → the next day

- tell 后必须接宾语，say 后可以不接宾语

## 知识点5：写作——故事复述/读后感

### 内容
**写作框架（故事复述）**：
1. 引言：介绍故事名称、作者、主要内容
2. 主体：按时间顺序复述主要情节
3. 结尾：简单评价

**写作框架（读后感）**：
1. 引言：介绍所读作品
2. 主体：介绍主要内容，表达感受，联系实际
3. 结尾：总结收获

**常用表达**：
- The story is about...
- The main character is...
- At the beginning/Middle/End of the story...
- What impresses me most is...
- I like this story because...
- From this story, I learn that...
- The story teaches us a lesson that...
