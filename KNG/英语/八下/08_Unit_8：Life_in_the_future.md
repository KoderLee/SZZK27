## 知识点2：重点句型

### 内容
**比较状语从句**：
- than 比
- as...as... 和……一样……
- not as/so...as... 不如……

**重点句型**：
- Life in the future will be better than it is now.
  未来的生活将比现在更好。
- Robots will be as intelligent as humans.
  机器人将和人类一样聪明。
- The city won't be as crowded as it is today.
  这座城市将不会像今天这样拥挤。
- The more technology develops, the better our life will be.
  科技发展得越快，我们的生活就会越好。

## 知识点3：语法——比较状语从句

### 内容
**than 引导的比较状语从句**：
- She is taller than I (am).
- He runs faster than I (do).

**as...as... 的用法**：
- 肯定句：as + 形容词/副词原级 + as
  - He is as tall as his father.
- 否定句：not as/so + 形容词/副词原级 + as
  - This book is not as/so interesting as that one.

**the + 比较级, the + 比较级**：
- The more you read, the more you will learn.
- The harder you work, the greater progress you will make.

**比较级的修饰词**：
- much, a lot, far, even, still, a little, a bit, slightly

### 常见题型

- 形容词/副词的比较级变化

### 注意事项
- 比较级中避免与自身比较
- 比较对象要一致
- 注意不规则比较级：good/well-better-best, bad-worse-worst

## 知识点5：写作——预测未来的生活

### 内容
**写作框架**：
1. 引言：总体预测未来生活的变化
2. 主体：分点描述各方面的变化（交通、通信、家居、工作等）
3. 结尾：表达对未来的期待或担忧

**常用表达**：
- In the future, I think/predict/believe...
- Life will be very different from what it is today.
- People will be able to...
- We won't have to... because...
- Technology will make our life easier/more convenient.
- I am looking forward to the future because...
- I hope the future will be...

**时态使用**：
- 一般将来时：will + 动词原形
- be going to + 动词原形
- may/might/could + 动词原形（表示可能性）
